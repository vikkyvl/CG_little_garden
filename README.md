# 🌱 Little Garden

A cozy pixel-art farming game built with Godot 4.6.

## About

Grow beautiful flowers, earn coins, and expand your garden! Take care of your flower beds, water plants, and unlock new flower varieties in the seed shop.

## How to Play

- **WASD / Arrow Keys** — move your character
- **Left Mouse Button** — water flowers, collect, plant, interact with UI
- Walk to an empty flower bed and click to choose a flower to plant
- Wait for the progress bar to fill, then water the flower
- Repeat until the flower is fully grown
- Collect your coins reward!

## Features

- 🌹 8 unique flowers: Rose, Tulip, Sunflower, Dandelion, Daisy, Bluebell, Iris, Orchid
- 🪙 Coin system — earn coins by growing flowers
- 🏪 Seed Shop — buy new flower varieties
- 💾 Auto-save — progress saves automatically
- 🎵 Background music and sound effects
- ☁️ Animated clouds and pixel-art UI
